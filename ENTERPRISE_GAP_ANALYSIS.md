## Critical Gaps & Mitigation Plans

### Gap 1: Email Marketing Automation
**Enterprise:** Advanced email campaigns, templates, A/B testing, drip sequences
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** Manual email via Gmail, no templates

**Impact:** Medium - SMS handles most communication, email is secondary
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 2):**
- Gmail API integration
- Pre-built email templates
- Automated email sequences (welcome, recall, review request)
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks post-MVP
**Cost:** $0 (Gmail API free tier sufficient)

---

### Gap 2: Advanced Reporting & Dashboards
**Enterprise:** Drag-and-drop report builder, custom dashboards, real-time charts
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** Predefined weekly/monthly reports, CSV export

**Impact:** Low - Predefined reports cover 90% of needs
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 2):**
- Custom date ranges
- Chart.js visualizations
- Export to PDF
- Real-time dashboard (top metrics)
**Timeline:** 1 week post-MVP
**Cost:** $0 (Chart.js open source)

---

### Gap 3: Multi-Location Support
**Enterprise:** Location hierarchies, territory management, location-specific workflows
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** Single location only

**Impact:** Low - Avatar Imaging is single location
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 3 - if needed):**
- Add `location_id` to contacts, bookings, tasks
- Location-specific staff assignments
- Per-location reporting
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks (if required)
**Cost:** $0 (D1 schema update)

---

### Gap 4: Granular Role Permissions
**Enterprise:** Field-level permissions, object-level security, custom permission sets
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** 3 roles (admin, staff, readonly)

**Impact:** Low - Small team, trust-based access
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 2):**
- Add permission matrix (view/edit/delete per object type)
- Field-level masking (hide sensitive data from readonly)
**Timeline:** 1 week
**Cost:** $0

---

### Gap 5: Mobile Native App
**Enterprise:** iOS/Android native apps with offline mode
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** Responsive web app only

**Impact:** Medium - Staff want mobile access
**Mitigation Plan (MVP):**
- Progressive Web App (PWA) - installable, works offline
- Mobile-optimized React UI
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 3):**
- React Native app (iOS + Android)
**Timeline:** PWA: 2 days | Native: 4-6 weeks
**Cost:** $0 (PWA) | $0 (React Native open source)

---

### Gap 6: HIPAA Compliance (BAA)
**Enterprise:** HIPAA-compliant by default, Business Associate Agreement (BAA) included
**Avatar CMS (MVP):** Cloudflare encryption, but no formal BAA

**Impact:** Medium-High - Medical data requires compliance
**Mitigation Plan (Phase 2):**
- Cloudflare HIPAA compliance available on Enterprise plan (~$200/month)
- Alternative: Self-host on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
- Consult healthcare compliance lawyer
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks (paperwork + review)
**Cost:** $200/month (Cloudflare Enterprise) OR $0 (self-host with encryption)

**Note:** For MVP, PII is phone numbers and names (not PHI). Actual medical records stay in Wix/clinic system.

## When Enterprise CRM Makes Sense

### Choose Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics if:

1. **Multi-product sales:** Selling multiple services, complex product catalog
2. **Long sales cycles:** 3-12 month enterprise deals, not appointments
3. **Large sales team:** 20+ salespeople needing territory management
4. **Complex integrations:** Need 50+ integrations out-of-the-box
5. **Regulatory:** Need guaranteed HIPAA/SOX/GDPR compliance with BAA
6. **Custom objects:** Need to track dozens of custom object types
7. **Advanced forecasting:** Revenue predictions, sales quotas, pipeline analytics

1. **Appointment-based:** Focus on booking and attending appointments
